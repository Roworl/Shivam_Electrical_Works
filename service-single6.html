Our Work Process

At Shivam Electrical, our compressor motor repair workflow is structured to ensure consistent, high-quality results and maximum motor efficiency:

  • Inspection & Testing: Conduct thorough examination of all motor components to detect faults.
  • Fault Diagnosis: Identify issues in windings, bearings, rotors, and electrical connections.
  • Precision Repair & Rewinding: Repair or replace damaged components and rewind motors accurately.
  • Balancing & Alignment: Ensure proper rotor balance and shaft alignment for smooth operation.
  • Quality Checks: Perform detailed inspections to verify efficiency, safety, and durability.
  • Final Performance Testing: Test motors under operational conditions to ensure peak performance.

This step-by-step workflow guarantees minimal downtime, improved efficiency, and long-lasting performance for all your compressor motors.
