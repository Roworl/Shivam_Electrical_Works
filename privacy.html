Privacy Policy

At Shivam Electrical, we value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the data you share with us while using our website or our electrical services. We ensure complete transparency and confidentiality in handling your personal and business-related details.

Collect Information

We collect only the information that is necessary to provide our services efficiently and securely. This may include your name, contact number, email address, company details, and project-related information when you reach out to us through our website, email, or phone. In addition, we may gather non-personal data such as browser type, IP address, and device information to improve website functionality and security. All collected information is used strictly for service-related and operational purposes, ensuring accuracy and reliability in our customer interactions.

Usage of Information

The information we collect is primarily used to provide, maintain, and enhance our services. We use your details to respond to your queries, deliver requested services, process transactions, and communicate important updates or promotional offers. Data collected may also be used for analytics to improve performance and ensure a better user experience. We never sell or share your personal information with third parties except as required by law or to provide services you have requested.

Additionally, collected data allows us to analyze trends, improve website functionality, and optimize our technical support. We may use non-personal information for statistical purposes, system maintenance, and service performance evaluation. Occasionally, we use your data to inform you about new services, offers, or policy changes always with the option to unsubscribe.

Your data is used responsibly, and we never sell or share it with third parties unless necessary for delivering services or fulfilling legal obligations.

Security Of User Data

We take data protection seriously. All personal information is stored securely and protected from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Our systems are updated regularly, and strict measures are in place to ensure your data remains safe. We also recommend users take precautions while sharing personal information online. Security measures include:
