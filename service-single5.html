Servo Motor Repair

At Shivam Electrical, we provide specialized servo motor repair and rewinding services for industrial and commercial applications. Our experienced technicians handle all types of servo motors, ensuring precise restoration and reliable operation. We specialize in fault diagnosis, winding repair, bearing replacement, encoder calibration, and performance tuning, giving your motors extended life and consistent, accurate operation. Every repair is carried out with meticulous attention to detail, using high-quality materials and following industry best practices for safety, efficiency, and precision.

We understand that equipment downtime can disrupt your operations, which is why our team works to quickly diagnose, repair, and optimize your servo motors. Our solutions are designed to be cost-effective, long-lasting, and customized to meet the unique requirements of each motor. From initial inspection to final testing, we ensure that every motor is restored to peak performance, providing reliability and smooth operation for your processes.

At the same time, our approach goes beyond just fixing faults — we also enhance the overall efficiency and accuracy of your servo motors. Through detailed inspection, precision rewinding, balancing, calibration, and performance optimization, we reduce energy consumption, minimize wear, and extend the service life of your equipment. This dedication to performance, reliability, and cost-effectiveness makes Shivam Electrical the trusted choice for businesses that require high-precision, high-performance servo motor solutions.

Our Work Process

At Shivam Electrical, we follow a structured workflow to ensure consistent, high-quality servo motor repairs. Our approach combines technical expertise with meticulous attention to detail to minimize downtime and maximize motor efficiency.This step-by-step workflow ensures minimal downtime, improved motor efficiency, and long-lasting performance, making your servo motors reliable and ready for industrial demands.

  • Inspection & Testing: Conduct thorough examination of all motor components to identify faults and ensure accurate diagnostics.
  • Fault Diagnosis: Detect issues in windings, bearings, encoders, and control mechanisms.
  • Precision Repair & Rewinding: Repair or replace damaged components and rewind motors with accuracy.
  • Calibration & Alignment: Adjust encoders and motor alignment for precise motion and performance.
  • Quality Checks: Perform detailed inspections to verify efficiency, safety, and operational accuracy.
  • Final Performance Testing: Test motors under operational conditions to ensure peak performance and precision.
